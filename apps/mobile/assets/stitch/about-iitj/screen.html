Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur

location_on The Permanent Campus

Spread over 852 acres of desert land, the IIT Jodhpur campus is a marvel of sustainable engineering and modern architecture. Located on National Highway 62, the campus is designed to be self-sufficient, featuring advanced research facilities, high-speed connectivity, and residential zones that harmonize with the Thar landscape.

Internal Transport

The campus provides regular electric shuttle services connecting hostels to academic blocks. Track live timings in the Transport section of this app.

restaurant

Dining & Mess

Hostels have dedicated mess facilities. For late-night cravings, the Students’ Activity Center (SAC) and various cafeteria outlets operate until midnight.

medical_services

Health Center

The campus health center is operational 24/7. Keep your student ID handy for all consultations and medication issuance.

wifi

Network Connectivity

Use your LDAP credentials to access the campus-wide high-speed Wi-Fi network. Academic buildings have superior coverage for research needs.
